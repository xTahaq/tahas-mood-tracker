HELP & DOCUMENTATION OF TAHA'S MOOD TRACKER

Getting Started

Hello! Thank you for using this app. You see the navigation bar at top of the website. You can go to any page at top right.
First, let's create a diary for today or any day! Click "Enter Data", choose today or custom (you have to choose date if it's custom). Fill the informations and click the Enter Data button.
You can view your data in "Statistics" page!

Data Management & Updating

Want to reset and clear your data? Go to settings and you will find Reset Data button.
Want to update? Go to tahas-mood-tracker github. Download the file. In case you lose your data, it is always smart to make backups! Go to Settings and press Copy Data button. Go to a text file and paste it there. You made a backup now, if you want to use that backup go to Settings and go to "Paste Data" section. Paste your data there and press "Paste" button. Deleting your browser history might delete your data.

Some warnings:

This app does not support yearly datas. For example if it's 2022 tomorrow, this app will stop working and you will need to update the app from github. And your data will no longer be available for use. It will not just fade out, when it's new year if you still want to view data from old year, copy your data from settings and go to a JSON formatter website and paste it in. You will somewhat see the old year's data there.
